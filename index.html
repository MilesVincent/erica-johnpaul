How it all began

Our Love Story

July 2021

First Meeting

A beautiful morning, beneath the quiet skies of Talalora, Samar the gentle rhythm of a Mass began to fill the air. Sunlight streamed through the windows as two hearts stood in the church, wrapped in a moment both sacred and still. In that serene hush, their story found its most beautiful beginning.

August 2021

First Date

They first met in a quiet church, where a simple hello turned into an easy, unexpected connection. Afterward, they strolled along the seawall, talking about their own history as the breeze and soft waves kept them company. Somewhere between the laughs and stories, something real began to grow.

3 Years later

The huge step

Years later, both hearts decided to step up in life and moved to a new place to make thier dreams come true. Lifting each other up through every challenge that comes thier way.

April 2026

The Proposal

In the quiet of Cebu Metropolitan Cathedral, he asked her to spend forever with him. With soft prayers in the air and hearts racing, the moment felt both intimate and sacred. In that familiar place, she said yes, and their story turned a beautiful new page.

September 2026

The Wedding

And now we arrive here — this time with family, laughter, and quiet anticipation. As they stood hand in hand, the journey that began with a simple meeting came full circle. Surrounded by love and promise, they say “I do” and step into forever together.
